    Return Steps

    If you decide to return to study at UH for a future semester, please do the following before returning to the U.S.:

    1. Contact your academic advisor to update your academic record or validate active student status.
    2. Contact ISSSOHLP@central.uh.edu to request a new Form I-20.
      • If you have to apply for readmission, your I-20 may come from admissions.
    3. Provide updated evidence of financial support.
    4. Verify/Update your foreign (international) address in MyUH.
    5. Your new Form I-20 will be sent by email. If you want the original by mail, please provide a prepaid mailing label.
    6. Pay the I-901 SEVIS Fee after receiving the new I-20. This is paid through www.fmjfee.com.
    7. Check with the U.S. Consulate or Embassy to determine if a new F-1 VISA is required.
